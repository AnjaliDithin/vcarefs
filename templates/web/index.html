Repatriation services

At your loved one’s loss in a foreign country, people are clueless about the further procedures, during such difficult times, we take care of everything from the start till the end until your loved one’s reaches the destination with great care and dignity.

Burial services

Burial facility is available in United Arab Emirates for expatriates. Burial Ground or emirate is decided on the bases of deceased visa Status; whether it is Residence/Visit etc and approval of local authorities. When we arrange burial, we ensure everything is followed as per the rules.

Cremation services

We arrange the cremation of your loved one as you request. In UAE we use different cremation centers located on different locations in different Emirates.We do also provide the services to repatriate of Ashes/Urns/Cremates of deceased to your requested destination.
